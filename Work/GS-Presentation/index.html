What really is a hybrid vehicle?
Answer is

ICE + Electric

Definition :

  • Car combining conventional combustion engine and electric motor powered by batteries
  • Electric power-train motors are present to provide better fuel economy than gasoline engine

    • Why use Hybrid Cars?

  • Greatly reduce fossil fuel dependance
  • Run on alternative fuel
  • Future ready
  • Nearly twice mileage and power
  • Greater fuel savings
  • Low pollution
